About Me

My name is GiGi! I am a native and reisdent of the DC metropolitan area, and I am an alumna of The George Washington University. And I love makeup! I am regularly flattered by requests for tutorials on how I do my eyeshadow. So while composing a final project for General Assembly's Font-End Wed Development course, I decided to kill two birds with one stone! I have carefully curated an assembly of tips, tricks, hacks, and tutorials for practical day-to-day use in applying makeup.

I want to emphasize that I am an AMATEUR. I am not a professional makeup artist by any means. I also want to be inclusive of other feature shades and shapes not my own, but I do not have the experience to bolster the tips I am curating here. If you feel you have a legitimate critique or valuable information to add, please email me. Thank you, and enjoy!
